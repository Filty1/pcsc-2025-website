Page Limit

For the Main Conference: Papers should have a minimum of 6 pages and maximum of 8 pages, including references.

For the Student Research Workshop: Papers should have a maximum of 4 pages in content with an unlimited number of pages for references and supplementary materials.


Preparing the Manuscript

For the Main Conference: This conference uses double-blind peer review. To prepare the blinded manuscript, here are the guidelines:

  1. Name and affiliation of the Authors must be removed from the submitted manuscript.
  2. Remove any citation and references that contain the Authors. Use [Anonymous, 2008] in citing the work of the Authors. For blinding the reference list, write '[Anonymous 2008] Details omitted for double-blind reviewing.'
  3. Do not include acknowledgment and funding sources.

For the Student Research Workshop: Submissions do not have to be anonymized.
