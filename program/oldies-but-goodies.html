Oldies but Goodies Session

Day 1 – May 9, 2024 (1:00 PM – 2:30 PM)

The "Oldies but Goodies" session will have invited speakers who will discuss and share how they have taught a specific Computer Science topic, over the years, in an interesting and insightful way.

In line with the 25th anniversary celebration of the founding of the Computing Society of the Philippines (CSP), the invited speakers of this special session are all pioneers of CSP.

▷  Moderator: Prof. Arnulfo P. Azcarraga, Ph.D.


Da Vinci code and Algorithm Design

Dr. Arnulfo P. Azcarraga

Dr. Arnulfo P. Azcarraga: PhD Computer Science from INP Grenoble, France, MS Computer Science from the Asian Institute of Technology (AIT), Bangkok, Thailand; BS Applied Mathematics, cum laude, from the University of the Philippines at Los Baños (UPLB). University Fellow and recently retired Professor of Computer Science at De La Salle University. Had been VC for Research, Associate VC for External Relations, Executive VP (Canlubang Campus), Dean, Graduate School Director, and Vice Dean of the College of Computer Studies. Had been a senior research fellow at the School of Computing, National University of Singapore, and currently a certified Senior Quality Assurance assessor and trainor of the ASEAN University Network. Published research in the theory and applications of self-organizing maps, artificial neural networks, and Generative AI. Member of the AI Council of PCIEERD/DOST. Most outstanding alumnus of UPLB (1996), of PSHS (Gawad Lagablab 2005), Ateneo de Zamboanga (2015), and of AIT (2018). A longtime member of Mensa.
