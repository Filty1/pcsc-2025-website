Call for Papers: 2024 Philippine Computing Science Congress

The 2024 Philippine Computing Science Congress is organized by the Computing Society of the Philippines to enable local and neighboring computing educators, researchers, information and communications technology (ICT) professionals, and students to interact and to share their work in computing, computer science, computational science, and ICT. The conference features special lectures by prominent researchers and educators and contributed papers in ICT, computing, computer science, computational science, and related disciplines.


Paper Submission

Submitted papers must be original, and not submitted concurrently to a journal or another conference/symposium. Each submitted paper will be peer-reviewed by at least two reviewers and must get an average positive rating for inclusion in the conference program and proceedings.

PCSC2024 will not accept any paper which, at the time of submission, is under review for or has already been published or accepted for publication in a journal or another conference. Authors are also required not to submit their papers elsewhere during PCSC2024 review period.

Papers must use the prescribed PCSC2024 templates.

Submission Portal: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/PCSC2024


Areas of Interest – Include (But are Not Limited to)

  • Natural Language Processing
  • Intelligent Systems (AI and Computational Intelligence)
  • Affective and Empathic Computing
  • Computational Theory and Algorithms
  • Modeling and Simulation
  • Computer Networks
  • Ubiquitous and Pervasive Computing
  • Software Engineering
  • Databases and Information
  • Databases and Information Retrieval Systems
  • Emerging Areas in Computing

Venue

The De La Salle University – Laguna Campus is a research university in Biñan, Laguna, envisioned to become a key resource for scientific and technological advancements in the country.
DLSU – Laguna Campus is located at Laguna Boulevard, LTI Spine Road, Barangays Biñan and Malamig, Biñan City, Laguna.
